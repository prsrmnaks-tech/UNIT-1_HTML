Natural Fertilizers

Feeding the soil naturally for healthy crops

Types of Natural Fertilizers

  • Compost: Decomposed organic matter
  • Manure: Animal waste rich in nutrients
  • Green Manure: Cover crops for soil fertility
  • Bone Meal: Supplies phosphorus and calcium
  • Fish Emulsion: Rich in nitrogen
  • Seaweed Fertilizer: Contains growth hormones

Advantages

  • Improves soil fertility
  • Enhances microorganism activity
  • Reduces pollution
  • Produces chemical-free food

Natural vs Chemical Fertilizers

Natural Fertilizers Chemical Properties
Eco-friendly Cause pollution
Improve soil health Damage soil structure
Release Nutrients slowly Release Nutrients quickly
Safe for humans and animals Harmful if misused
